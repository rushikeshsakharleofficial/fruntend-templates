# Stripe Design Profile

## Best use cases
Pricing, billing, subscription, payment, fintech, checkout, and revenue dashboards.

## Color tokens
- Background: white / dark navy
- Accent: purple, indigo, blue gradients
- Success: green
- Warning: amber
- Danger: red
- Border: soft neutral

## Typography
Polished SaaS typography with readable product copy and clear pricing hierarchy.

## Buttons
Strong primary CTA, clean secondary button, clear upgrade/payment actions.

## Cards
Pricing cards, plan comparison sections, billing panels, invoice rows, usage cards.

## Layout
Conversion-focused landing sections plus billing console patterns.

## Prompt pattern
Generate a Stripe-style billing or pricing interface with polished typography, gradient accents, pricing cards, invoices, usage meters, and responsive layout.
